## 📋 **S&OP Project Overview**

A comprehensive **Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP) analytics platform** analyzing AD Solutions' complete operations across 30 products, 4,800+ orders, and 5 South African regions. This mini-project demonstrates enterprise-grade S&OP capabilities from demand forecasting to inventory optimization and operational performance tracking, showcasing integrated planning excellence.

<td width="50%">

- **Data Scale:** 4,800+ orders, 30 products
- **Timeline:** September 2025 Operations
- **Geography:** 5 SA regions (Western Cape, Gauteng, KZN, Eastern Cape, Free State)
- **Framework:** Enterprise S&OP analytics architecture

</td>
<td width="50%">
